## Task 1
1.  If the sampling rate is lower than twice the highest frequency of target signal, there will be aliasing, unwanted periodical signal appears. In digital image, the repetition is in space rather than time. If a repetitive pattern of high spatial frequency is sampled at low resolution, Moire patterns result. 
2.  Jittering is simply randomazing the sampling patterns which can lead to display the moire patterns as noises. The reason why they are only visible to a reduced extent is frequency of the signal is lower and sampling frequency is enough to recover for that part, there wont be any noises that is resulted from the jittering of Moire artifacts.
